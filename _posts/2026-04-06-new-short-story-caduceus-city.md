I'm excited to share my latest short story, [Caduceus City](https://randylubin.com/fiction/caduceus-city.html), which was commissioned by [Summer of Protocols](https://protocolized.summerofprotocols.com/). They asked me to explore the trade-offs between [rooms and cyborgs](https://interconnected.org/home/2025/10/13/dichotomy) as different near-future approaches to computing and interface design. A source of inspiration was [this Dynamicland video](https://dynamicland.org/2022/Biomolecular_design_in_Realtalk/) where they prototype room-scale interfaces for biomolecular design.
